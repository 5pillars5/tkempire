The Protocol

Four Phases Back

The Never Give Up Protocol is not a trade. It is a systematic process. Four phases. Each one building on the last. No shortcuts. No revenge. Just the machine working.

Phase01
Stop the Bleeding
Before you recover anything you have to stop losing. This phase is about total emotional detachment from trading. No live trades. No margin. No leverage. Just assessment.
Calculate your exact loss — total capital lost, percentage down from peak
Close all open positions that are bleeding — take the loss, stop the damage
Set your recovery capital — only deploy what you can afford to lose again
Timeline: 48–72 hours of no trading. Just planning.
Phase02
Deploy the Grid
This is where the Never Give Up Protocol starts working. Deploy a tight trailing grid bot on a high-liquidity pair. Small capital. Low leverage. Let the machine collect.
Start with 10–20% of remaining capital only — protect the rest
Pair selection: ETH/USDT or BTC/USDT — highest liquidity, most grid cycles
Grid settings: 10–15 levels, 4–6% tight range, continuous trailing mode
Leverage: 1x to 3x maximum — recovery is not the time for aggressive leverage
Target: 5–8% monthly return on deployed capital through grid cycles
Phase03
Compound the Returns
Once the grid is running and producing consistent returns for 30 days, you begin compounding. Profits from the grid fund the next grid deployment. Capital grows systematically.
Reinvest 70% of grid profits back into the bot — compound the recovery
Keep 30% as a cash reserve — psychological safety net
Add a second grid on a different pair after 60 days of consistency
Track every cycle — the log becomes your proof of progress
Do not increase leverage until capital is 50% recovered
Phase04
Return to Full Operation
When your capital has recovered to 80%+ of pre-loss levels and the grid has been running consistently for 90 days, you are ready to return to full trading operation — this time with the grid running in the background permanently.
Resume Empire Signals trades with full position sizing
Keep the recovery grid running — it becomes your passive income layer
Set new capital protection rules — the loss taught you something, use it
You are no longer recovering. You are operating. The empire is back.

Protocol Rules

The 8 Rules of Recovery

Break one of these and you are no longer following the protocol. You are revenge trading with extra steps.

1

Never Increase Position Size to Recover Faster

The protocol works at its own pace. Rushing it by adding size is how a 40% loss becomes a 90% loss.

2

Maximum 3x Leverage During Recovery

You lost capital because of risk. This is not the time for 10x or 15x. The grid at 1–3x still compounds. It just takes longer. That is the point.

3

Deploy Maximum 20% of Remaining Capital

You need a psychological reserve. Knowing 80% of your capital is safe lets the bot run without panic. Deploy small. Let it prove itself first.

4

No Manual Trades for 30 Days

The first 30 days are bot only. Your emotions are not ready. The machine is. Let it work while you reset your mindset completely.

5

Log Every Grid Cycle

Watching the bot collect small wins daily rebuilds your confidence systematically. The log becomes proof that the protocol is working even when it feels slow.

6

Reinvest 70% — Keep 30% Cash

Compounding accelerates recovery. But keeping 30% liquid protects you from needing to liquidate the bot if life happens. Protect the machine.

7

Shift the Grid When Price Trends

The trailing grid adjusts automatically. Trust the technology. Do not manually override the bot because you think you know where price is going. You thought that before too.

8

Define Your Recovery Target Before You Start

Know the number you are recovering to. Without a target the protocol has no finish line. Set it. Write it down. Work the protocol until you hit it.
